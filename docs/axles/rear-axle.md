# OM617TJ Rear Axle

## Overview

The rear axle setup for the OM617TJ is based around a Ford 8.8 rear axle sourced from a Ford Explorer. The axle was selected for its strength, factory disc brakes, large axle shafts, and strong aftermarket support.

This axle has been upgraded with reinforced housing components, upgraded axle shafts, lower gearing, and a selectable locker to handle the increased torque output and off-road abuse associated with the OM617 diesel drivetrain and 37-inch tires.

# Axle Reinforcement

## Clayton Offroad Truss

The rear axle housing is reinforced using a Clayton Offroad truss system.

### Purpose
- Increase housing rigidity
- Reduce axle tube flex
- Strengthen suspension mounting points
- Improve durability under heavy articulation and torque loads

### Notes
- Fully welded truss system
- Designed for long-arm suspension systems
- Helps maintain gear alignment under load

### Strength
- Strong factory axle housing
- Large 8.8-inch ring gear
- 31-spline axle shafts

### Factory Disc Brakes
- Better braking performance than many factory Jeep rear axles
- Simplified maintenance

### Aftermarket Support
- Wide availability of gears, lockers, shafts, and rebuild parts
- Strong support within the Jeep off-road community

### Cost Effectiveness
- High strength-to-cost ratio
- Common and easy to source
