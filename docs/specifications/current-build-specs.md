# Current Build Specifications

## Vehicle

- 2005 Jeep Wrangler TJ

## Engine / Induction

- Mercedes OM617 turbo diesel
- Holset HE221W turbocharger with 7cm housing
- Boost target: approximately 30 PSI
- Water-to-air intercooler system
- Injection pump: 7.5mm current setup with planned 8mm M-pump upgrade

## Fuel System

- Dual-fuel layout: diesel + waste oil
- Driver-side tank: diesel
- Passenger-side tank: waste oil
- Primary filters: Baldwin BF1272-SPS per tank
- Final fuel filter: Donaldson P551313
- Engine oil filter: Baldwin B2-HPG

## Transmission

- Novak AX15

## Transfer Case

- Dana 300

## Tires / Wheels

- 37-inch Toyo tires
- Vision Manx beadlock wheels

## Suspension

- Clayton long arms
- Travel King 2.0 coilovers

## Axles / Lockers

- OX air lockers

## Electrical Baseline

- Dual AGM batteries, 800 CCA each
- Blue Sea battery selector
- 500A breakers and ANL protection for major loads
- AD244 alternator

## Build Priorities

1. Reliability
2. Simplicity where possible
3. Real-world performance under load
4. Field serviceability
