# Lighting Overview

## Front Lighting

- Generic 7-inch LED headlights
- Diode Dynamics SSC2 forward-facing fog lights
- Diode Dynamics SSC1 angled side lights
- Diode Dynamics SS5 bumper lights
- Diode Dynamics SSC1 Pro knuckle lights

## Rear Lighting

- Xprite chase light bar
- Diode Dynamics C1R brake / running / turn lights

## System Intent

The lighting package supports normal road legality plus trail visibility, side illumination, bumper-forward lighting, and rear visibility/signaling.

## Real Engineering Concerns

- Separation between road-legal lighting circuits and auxiliary lighting circuits
- Switch logic and driver ergonomics
- Fuse and relay placement for field service
- Ground quality and voltage drop at LED loads
- Wire routing around abrasion, water, and front-end heat
