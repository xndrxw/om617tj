# Turbo System Overview

## Current Turbo

- Holset HE221W
- 7cm turbine housing
- Internal wastegate
- Boost target: approximately 30 PSI

## Supporting Hardware

- Mishimoto boost controller
- Wrapped 3-inch downpipe
- Water-to-air intercooler system tied into the FrozenBoost / Meziere package

## System Intent

The turbo package is aimed at strong usable torque and power from the OM617 without turning the build into a fragile high-strung setup.

## Current Watch Areas

- Wastegate and boost-control stability near the upper target
- Sustained EGT under heavy load
- Spool behavior relative to fueling rate
- Downpipe heat management near surrounding components
- Drivetrain load once boost and fuel are both pushed upward

## What Is Known vs Unknown

Known:
- The turbo family, housing size, wastegate style, and approximate boost target are already established.
- The build uses water-to-air intercooling and a wrapped 3-inch downpipe.

Still not fully documented in the repo:
- Exact wastegate-control plumbing
- Boost-controller settings
- Measured spool / response notes
- EGT behavior by load and ambient conditions
